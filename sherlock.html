"Sherlock Holmes"refers to the fictional consulting detective created by sir Arthur Conan Doyle.

The character first appeared in the novel A Study in Scarlet in 1887 and became widely popular through short stories published in The Strand Magazine beginning in 1891.

These stories, later collected as The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes, feature Holmes and his companion Dr. Watson solving mysteries using Holmes's exceptional deductive reasoning and observational skills.
