"The Alchemist," by Paulo Coelho, is a philosophical novel published in 1988 that follows Santiago, a young shepherd from Spain, on a journey to fulfill his dream of finding treasure near the Egyptian pyramids

The book explores themes of destiny, personal legend, and the importance of following one's heart and listening to omens.
